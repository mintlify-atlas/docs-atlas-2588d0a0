Savings management in OpenCBS Cloud provides comprehensive tools for managing client deposit accounts, processing transactions, and tracking balances.

## Understanding Savings Accounts

Savings accounts in OpenCBS Cloud are configured through savings products that define:

- Interest rates and calculation methods
- Fee structures (deposit, withdrawal, management, entry, close)
- Minimum balance requirements
- Account restrictions and rules
- Transaction types allowed

## Creating a Savings Account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate from Client Profile">
    Open the client profile (person or company) and click **Create Savings Account** from the profile actions menu.
    
    <Note>
    Savings accounts must be linked to a client profile. You cannot create standalone savings accounts.
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select Savings Product">
    Choose the appropriate savings product from the dropdown. The product determines:
    
    - Interest rate range
    - Fee structure and limits
    - Account features and restrictions
    
    The form automatically loads product-specific parameters.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Configure Account Parameters">
    Set the savings account parameters:
    
    **Interest Rate**
    - Select rate within the product's allowed range
    - Rate is typically annual percentage
    - May be fixed based on product configuration
    
    **Open Date**
    - Date the account becomes active
    - Defaults to current date
    - Affects interest calculation start
    
    **Savings Officer**
    - Assign responsible staff member
    - Defaults to current user
    - Can be changed later
    
    **Fee Configuration**
    - Deposit fees (rate and flat)
    - Withdrawal fees (rate and flat)
    - Entry fees (one-time opening fees)
    - Close fees (account closure fees)
    - Management fees (periodic fees)
    
    <Tip>
    Fees are pre-configured by the savings product but can be adjusted within allowed min/max ranges. Fields may be disabled if the product uses fixed fee amounts.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review and Submit">
    Review all account parameters and click **Submit** to create the savings account. The account is immediately available for transactions.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Viewing Savings Account Information

Access comprehensive account information from the savings account screen:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Account Summary">
    The **Info** tab displays:
    
    - Account number and status
    - Current balance
    - Available balance
    - Interest accrued but not posted
    - Account opening date
    - Assigned savings officer
    - Interest rate
    - Fee structure
    
    Key balance indicators:
    - **Current Balance**: Total funds in account
    - **Available Balance**: Funds available for withdrawal (may differ due to holds)
    - **Minimum Balance**: Required minimum (from product)
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Transaction History">
    Navigate to the **Entries** tab to view:
    
    - All deposits and withdrawals
    - Interest postings
    - Fee deductions
    - Balance after each transaction
    - Transaction dates and times
    - Performing user
    - Transaction descriptions
    
    Use pagination to browse historical transactions.
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Interest Details">
    View interest calculation details:
    
    - Interest rate applied
    - Accrued interest (not yet posted)
    - Interest posting schedule
    - Posted interest transactions
    - Interest calculation method
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Processing Deposits

Deposit funds into a savings account:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Deposit Function">
    From the main menu, navigate to **Teller Management** → **Teller Operations**, or access deposits directly from the savings account.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter Deposit Details">
    Complete the deposit form:
    
    **Account Selection**
    - Search for and select the savings account
    - Verify client information
    
    **Amount**
    - Enter deposit amount
    - System shows deposit fee (if applicable)
    - Total credit = Amount - Deposit fee
    
    **Date**
    - Transaction date (defaults to current date)
    - Affects interest calculation
    
    **Payment Method**
    - Cash
    - Bank transfer
    - Check
    - Mobile money
    
    **Description/Notes**
    - Add transaction description
    - Reference numbers
    - Any special notes
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review and Process">
    The system displays:
    - Deposit amount
    - Applicable fees
    - Net credit to account
    - New balance (projected)
    
    Click **Process Deposit** to complete the transaction.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Print Receipt">
    After processing:
    - Transaction confirmation appears
    - Print receipt for client
    - Receipt shows transaction details and new balance
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
**Deposit Best Practices:**
- Always verify the account number before processing
- Count cash deposits twice
- Print receipts for client records
- Enter reference numbers for non-cash deposits
- Add notes for unusual transactions
</Tip>

## Processing Withdrawals

Withdraw funds from a savings account:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Withdrawal Function">
    Navigate to **Teller Operations** or access from the savings account directly.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter Withdrawal Details">
    Complete the withdrawal form:
    
    **Account Selection**
    - Search and select savings account
    - System displays current balance
    
    **Amount**
    - Enter withdrawal amount
    - Must not exceed available balance
    - System shows withdrawal fee (if applicable)
    - Total debit = Amount + Withdrawal fee
    
    **Verification**
    - Verify client identity
    - Check signature card (if applicable)
    - Confirm withdrawal authorization
    
    **Payment Method**
    - Cash payout
    - Bank transfer
    - Check
    
    <Warning>
    The system prevents withdrawals that would bring the balance below the minimum required balance defined in the savings product.
    </Warning>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Process Withdrawal">
    Review withdrawal summary:
    - Withdrawal amount
    - Applicable fees
    - New balance after withdrawal
    
    Click **Process Withdrawal** to complete.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Complete Transaction">
    - System confirms transaction
    - Print receipt for client
    - Disburse cash or initiate transfer
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Interest Calculation and Posting

OpenCBS Cloud automatically calculates and posts interest based on the savings product configuration:

### Interest Accrual

Interest accrues:
- **Daily**: Based on daily balance and annual rate
- **Automatically**: No manual intervention required
- **Visible**: Shows in account summary as "Accrued Interest"

### Interest Posting

Interest is posted to the account:
- **Periodically**: According to product schedule (monthly, quarterly, annually)
- **Automatically**: Through scheduled jobs
- **Transaction Created**: Posted interest appears in transaction history

### Viewing Interest

To view interest details:
1. Open savings account
2. Navigate to **Entries** tab
3. Filter for "Interest" transaction types
4. See posting dates and amounts

<Note>
Interest calculation methods (simple, compound) and posting frequencies are configured in the savings product and cannot be changed at the account level.
</Note>

## Account Fees

Savings accounts may have various fees:

### Fee Types

**Entry Fee**
- One-time fee charged when account is opened
- Can be percentage or flat amount
- Deducted from initial deposit or charged separately

**Deposit Fee**
- Charged on each deposit transaction
- Can be percentage of deposit or flat amount
- Deducted from deposit amount

**Withdrawal Fee**
- Charged on each withdrawal transaction
- Can be percentage of withdrawal or flat amount
- Added to withdrawal amount

**Management Fee**
- Periodic fee (monthly, annually)
- Charged for account maintenance
- Automatically deducted from balance

**Close Fee**
- One-time fee when account is closed
- Deducted from final balance

### Fee Visibility

All fees are:
- Shown before transaction processing
- Recorded in transaction history
- Included in account statements
- Reported in fee reports

## Closing Savings Accounts

To close a savings account:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Close Function">
    Open the savings account and navigate to **Operations** → **Close Account**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Verify Closure Eligibility">
    System checks:
    - Account balance
    - Pending transactions
    - Linked products or services
    
    <Warning>
    Some accounts cannot be closed if they are linked to active loans or other products. Resolve dependencies first.
    </Warning>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Process Closure">
    Enter closure details:
    - Close date
    - Closure reason
    - Final withdrawal method (if balance exists)
    - Close fee application
    
    System calculates:
    - Final accrued interest
    - Close fees
    - Final balance to disburse
  </Step>

  <Step title="Confirm and Complete">
    Review final account summary and click **Close Account**. The account status changes to Closed and no further transactions are allowed.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Savings Account Transfers

Transfer funds between savings accounts:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Transfer Function">
    Navigate to **Transfers** from the main menu.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select Source and Destination">
    **Source Account**
    - Search and select the account to debit
    - Verify sufficient balance
    
    **Destination Account**
    - Search and select the account to credit
    - Can be same or different client
    - Verify account is active
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter Transfer Details">
    - Transfer amount
    - Transfer date
    - Description/reference
    - Any applicable fees
  </Step>

  <Step title="Process Transfer">
    Click **Process Transfer**. The system:
    - Debits source account
    - Credits destination account
    - Records both transactions
    - Creates accounting entries
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
Internal transfers between accounts are typically instant. External transfers may require additional processing time depending on the payment method.
</Note>

## Savings Account Status

Accounts can have different statuses:

- **Active**: Account is operational, transactions allowed
- **Inactive**: Account dormant but not closed
- **Pending**: Account created but not yet approved
- **Closed**: Account permanently closed

Status changes are tracked in the account events history.

## Bulk Operations

For multiple accounts or clients:

### Bulk Interest Posting

- Automatically posts interest for all eligible accounts
- Runs on scheduled basis (configured by admin)
- Can be triggered manually for specific accounts

### Bulk Fee Collection

- Collects periodic fees from all accounts
- Scheduled based on product configuration
- Creates individual transactions for each account

## Reporting on Savings

Access savings reports:

- **Account Balances**: Current balances for all accounts
- **Transaction Summary**: Deposit and withdrawal totals
- **Interest Posted**: Interest payment reports
- **Fee Collection**: Fee revenue reports
- **Dormant Accounts**: Inactive account analysis

Navigate to **Reports** → **Savings Reports** to generate these reports.

## Troubleshooting Common Issues

<Warning>
**Common Errors:**

**"Insufficient balance"**
- Withdrawal amount + fees exceed available balance
- Check minimum balance requirements
- Verify no holds on the account

**"Account not active"**
- Account may be pending approval
- Account may be closed or suspended
- Check account status

**"Withdrawal limit exceeded"**
- Product may have daily/monthly withdrawal limits
- Check product configuration
- Contact administrator if limits need adjustment
</Warning>
